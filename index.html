Skills

Product thinking

Digital products live or die by turning insights into products that solve problems efficiently, satisfy customers, and delight users. I leverage methods to perform qualitative and quantitative analyses to measure success.

Software and hardware make up the technical or magical side of digital products; I strive to get a deep understanding of the technology involved in whatever project I’m working on.

Interaction design

Digital products must be intuitive, meet the user where and how the user expects them, and get out of the way when they are no longer needed.

I use effective tools to convert the product requirements into simple and elegant user interface systems:

  • Interviews and workshops to capture the requirements of stakeholders.
  • Flow chart diagrams to map user flows using Figjam.
  • UI prototype using Figma or Adobe XD.
  • Web UI components using Visual Studio Code + HTML, CSS, JS and React.

Visual design

The first impressions matter and most of this sentiment are derived from the form, perfomarnce, and aesthetics of the product. I use tools to achieve high resolution and detail so that users feel constantly engaged.

  • UI components library using Figma or Adobe XD.
  • Illustration and iconography using Adobe Illustrator and Fresco.
  • Animation and special effects using Adobe After Effects.
  • Photo editing and graphics for print using Adobe Photoshop.

Proactivity, intentionality & self-awareness

Genuine respect and kindness are the best way to get your foot into almost anybody’s office. Projects tend to run a lot smoother when the relevant stakeholders are included and communicated about the initiatives and progress. I go out there and talk to stakeholders and users to get a first-hand understanding of the priorities and feasibility of the design options. I focus on getting to the root of problems to understand the impact, consequences, and motivations.

I understand that product design and development is a marathon and not a sprint. Life-Work balance is a priority to create an environment where creativity and productivity can thrive. Like products, the people behind them are always evolving; the goal is to create a virtuous cycle where talent, health, and peace can expand.
